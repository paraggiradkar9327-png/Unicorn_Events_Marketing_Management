Managing Director & Founder

Mr. Narendra Atkare

Mr. Narendra Atkare is the visionary leader behind Unicorn Events, known for his practical approach and creative mindset. With a strong passion for planning and precision, he has built Unicorn Event Management & Marketing Pvt Ltd into a trusted name for large scale events and branding solutions. Under his guidance, the company has successfully delivered government projects, corporate events, and public programs across India. His focus on quality, teamwork, and meaningful execution ensures every event creates a lasting impact.

"
An event is not something that happens to people. It is something that happens inside them. Our job is to engineer that experience with the same rigour and intention that an architect brings to a building — except our structure must stand for only one night, and be remembered for a lifetime.
— Narendra Atkare, Founder & Managing Director

A respected leader and creative force in the events and entertainment industry, Narendra Atkare has built a strong reputation for delivering impactful experiences across product launches, corporate conferences, wedding branding, and brand activation campaigns. Based in Nagpur, Narendra is known for combining strategic thinking with innovative event execution, helping brands and organizations create memorable audience engagement.

With extensive experience in managing large-scale events and experiential marketing initiatives, Narendra has successfully led numerous high-profile projects for corporate clients, social celebrations, and promotional campaigns. His expertise spans concept development, event production, branding strategy, audience engagement, and on-ground activation management.

Beyond his professional accomplishments, Narendra is passionate about elevating the standards of the live events industry through creativity, professionalism, and meaningful brand storytelling. His commitment to delivering seamless and impactful experiences has made him a trusted name in the event management and entertainment space.

Leadership Philosophy

How He Leads

◈

Lead by Example

Narendra is known for being present on every major production — not micromanaging, but demonstrating the standard of attention and care he expects from his entire team.

◉

Hire for Character

Skills can be taught. Character cannot. He built Unicorn Events on a team of people who are as committed to the client's success as they are talented at their craft.

◎

Challenge Every Brief

His first question to every client is never "how?" — it is always "why?" Understanding the purpose behind an event produces far superior outcomes than simply executing instructions.

◇

Celebrate Honestly

After every production, the team conducts a full debrief — not just to celebrate what went right, but to honestly examine every imperfection and turn it into a lesson.

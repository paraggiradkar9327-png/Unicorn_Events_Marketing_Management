Our Journey

A Decade of Defining Moments

2012

The Beginning

Founded in Los Angeles with a bold vision — to bring a new standard of excellence to the events industry. Our first corporate gala served 200 guests. Every detail was handled with the same care we bring to events of 20,000 today.

2015

International Expansion

Opened offices in London and Dubai, extending our reach to the Middle East and Europe. Our first international production — a three-day leadership conference in Abu Dhabi — earned our first industry award for excellence.

2018

Entertainment Division

Launched our dedicated entertainment management division — end-to-end artist representation, talent booking, and live show production. Within two years, 50+ live entertainment events across four continents.

2021

Hybrid Innovation

When the world changed, so did we. We pioneered a hybrid event model combining broadcast-quality live streaming with in-person experiences — helping over 80 clients maintain momentum through global disruption.

2024+

The Next Chapter

Today, 150+ active clients, offices on five continents, 200+ team specialists, and a global reputation as one of the most sought-after event and entertainment agencies in the world.

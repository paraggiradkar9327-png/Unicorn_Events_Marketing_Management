Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I contact you?
For large-scale productions, we recommend engaging our India-based team at least 6–12 months in advance to ensure seamless planning and execution. For smaller or mid-sized events, a lead time of 3–6 months is typically sufficient to maintain our quality standards and deliver a well-managed experience.
Do you have a minimum budget requirement?
Our productions are tailored to the scale and requirements of each event, ranging from smaller corporate gatherings to large flagship experiences. We maintain complete transparency on what can be achieved within your budget and expectations before any commitment is made, ensuring clarity and confidence at every stage.
Can you produce events outside your office locations?
While our operations are based from a single office in India, we have successfully delivered events across multiple locations and built strong on-ground vendor relationships. Wherever your event takes place, we ensure consistent quality and execution to our standards.
Do you provide talent and entertainment booking?
Yes. Our entertainment management division handles artist representation, talent booking, and live performance production. We have direct relationships with A-list performers, keynote speakers, and entertainment acts worldwide — removing the need for a separate agency.
What happens after I submit an enquiry?
A senior member of our production team will contact you within 24 hours to schedule an initial consultation. This is a no-obligation conversation where we learn about your vision, answer your questions, and assess how we can best support your project.
Do you offer virtual and hybrid event production?
Yes — we have extensive experience producing hybrid and fully virtual events to broadcast quality. From live streaming infrastructure and virtual platforms to interactive audience experiences, we ensure your digital audience receives an equally immersive experience.
